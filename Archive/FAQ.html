Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find an answer? Don't hesitate to reach out!

How do I know the mentors are legit?

Great question, all of our mentors are ran through our rigorous 15 point system, they fill out a questionnaire that we score, we then follow up with a welcome call to ensure the highest level of mentorship.

Why would I want a mentor, I can google it can't I?

Much like yourself, our founders "googled" a lot of their troubles. But someone who has been in your situation and give you answers and feedback based on your situation is priceless and increases your likely hood of success incredibly. (Google can't hold your hand!)

How do I know this is right for me?

We have mentees from all walks of life, some work at the local movie theater while others work at fortune 500 companies (even other company founders use us!), you can never know too much and you can never get enough advice from the right people. Mentors can and will change your life.
